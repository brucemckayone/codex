the descent

Every journey walks the same five stages — not a curriculum to complete, but a path the body learns to travel.

iRegulationFinding the ground. Teaching the body it is safe enough to soften.
iiEmbodimentComing back into sensation — feeling what is actually here.
iiiAttunementListening inward; letting need and impulse be known.
ivCo-regulationBeing met by another. The body settling in company.
vReclamationLiving from wholeness — taking back what was held away.
