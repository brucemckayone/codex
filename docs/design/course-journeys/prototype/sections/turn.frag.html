The five stages

There is a way down. It begins at the root.

Rootwork is a descent — five stages the body walks to find its ground again. You don't climb toward calm. You settle into it.

  1. i

    Regulation

    Finding the ground. Teaching the body it is safe enough to soften.

  2. ii

    Embodiment

    Coming back into sensation — feeling what is actually here.

  3. iii

    Attunement

    Listening inward; letting need and impulse be known.

  4. iv

    Co-regulation

    Being met by another. The body settling in company.

  5. v

    Reclamation

    Living from wholeness — taking back what was held away.
