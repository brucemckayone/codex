# Brand Editor — Admin User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Studio > Settings > Branding**
2. Click **"Edit Brand Live"**
3. The brand editor panel opens over your live site

## The Editor Panel

The floating panel appears in the bottom-right corner. It has three parts:

- **Header**: Navigation breadcrumb, theme toggle, minimize/close buttons
- **Content**: The editing controls (scrollable)
- **Footer**: Unsaved indicator, Reset and Save buttons

## Editing Your Brand

### Colors

The **Colors** screen lets you set four colors:

- **Primary**: Your main brand color. Used for buttons, links, and interactive elements. Gets a full color picker with canvas area and hue slider
- **Secondary**: A supporting color for secondary elements
- **Accent**: A highlight color for badges, featured items, and CTAs
- **Background**: Optional custom page background (clear to use default)

Use **"Fine-tune colors..."** to override individual derived colors (interactive hover, focus ring, text-on-brand contrast).

### Typography

Choose **body** and **heading** fonts from the available Google Fonts. A live preview shows how text looks with each font.

Use **"Fine-tune typography..."** to adjust text scale (0.85x - 1.15x) and font weights.

### Shape & Spacing

- **Corner Radius**: Sharp (0rem) to Playful (1rem) — affects buttons, cards, inputs
- **Density**: Compact (0.85x) to Spacious (1.15x) — scales all spacing

### Shadows

Preview your current shadow styles. Full shadow customization coming soon.

### Logo

Upload your organization logo. Accepts PNG, SVG, or WebP (max 2MB). The preview shows the logo against your current brand background.

## Presets

Four built-in presets on the home screen for quick starting points:

| Preset | Style |
|--------|-------|
| **Minimal** | Clean, sharp corners, neutral palette |
| **Vibrant** | Bold, rounded, saturated colors |
| **Corporate** | Professional, tight spacing |
| **Dark** | Moody, dark surface, light accents |

Click a preset to apply it instantly. You can then fine-tune from there.

## Saving & Discarding

- **Save**: Persists your changes to the server. All visitors see the new brand
- **Reset**: Reverts to the last saved state (discards unsaved changes)
- **Close (with changes)**: Prompts to confirm discard
- **Navigate away (with changes)**: Browser shows "Leave site?" warning

Unsaved changes persist in your browser session — if you refresh, the editor reopens with your pending changes.

## Theme Preview

Click the **◑** button in the header to toggle between light and dark mode preview. This lets you see how your brand looks in both themes before saving. The toggle reverts to the original theme when you close the editor.

## Mobile

On screens below 640px, the editor becomes a full-width bottom sheet (max 70% of viewport height).

## Tips

- Start with a preset, then customize
- Use the theme toggle to check both light and dark appearance
- The "Fine-tune" screens give per-token control for advanced customization
- Changes are live — scroll the page behind the editor to see the effect
